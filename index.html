About Us

TRASBASH by KALTANI

A clean-tech circular economy recycling company that empowers individuals to become active participants in building a more sustainable future. This is achieved via our innovative mobile application, designed to catalyze plastic and aluminum waste aggregation from the palm of your hands.
This mobile application enhances waste aggregation and collection efforts by encouraging responsible waste disposal, consequently reducing carbon emissions, combating ocean plastic pollution, and creating ethical job opportunities.
